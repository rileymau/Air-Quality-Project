About this website

Login as a user, and then do an Air quality search. Air Quality Index (AQI) has many measurements.
Two important ones are ground level ozone (ozone), and particle matter 2.5 micrometers or smaller (PM 2.5).

A variety of air quality data is available. This website searches for ozone and PM 2.5 data by
zipcode or by reporting area. Cities and towns are assigned to larger reporting areas that share measurements.
The extended search pages has a summary of the last year's AQI's for any zipcode or reporting area.
