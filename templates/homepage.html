Welcome to Air Quality Zipcode Lookup Page



About This Website:

Have you ever wanted to look up air quality data before you traveled? Would you like to know more about
the air quality where you live? Search by zipcode on our website, that gets data from AirNow.gov.

Air Quality Index (AQI) has many measurements. Two important ones are ground level ozone (ozone),
and particle matter 2.5 micrometers or smaller (PM 2.5). This website shows current AQI levels by zipcode,
AQI levels from the previous week, and AQI levels on previous days you would like to look up. You can also see
a summary of all searches done for each zipcode.

For more information on AQI and these measurements: AirNow API AQI Information
